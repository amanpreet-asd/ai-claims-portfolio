Case Study

This is a concise story of the product. It covers the problem, the vision, the people we serve, the solution concept, and how we measure success.

Claim handling in personal lines often moves slower than it should. People enter and verify data by hand, which leads to delays and inconsistent service. Errors happen, which can create overpayment or underpayment and a poor experience for customers and brokers.
My vision is clear. Use an AI powered assistant to automate common steps so people can focus on complex cases. The aim is to automate half or more of repetitive work, shorten cycle time, and raise quality without losing human judgment where it matters.
Brokers
Need quick updates and a simple way to share status with clients.
Adjusters
Need more time for complex judgment and less time on data entry.
Claimants
Want fair outcomes and fast resolution with clear communication.

Key capabilities I would deliver first.

  • Document intake with extraction of key data and automatic validation
  • Risk and completeness flags for missing or unusual details
  • Suggested payout ranges based on similar past cases and policy terms
  • Simple views for brokers, adjusters, and leaders with clear status

Human review stays for high risk or high value claims. Automation speeds up standard cases.

Cycle Time
Track average days from receipt to settlement. Goal is a clear drop after the pilot.
Quality
Fewer rework events and fewer payout corrections show better accuracy.
Experience
Customer and broker feedback improves when updates are quick and clear.

Sample Product Backlog

A small set of epics with one user story written out in full to show clarity and testability.

Epic. Claims submission automation

User story. As a broker I want to upload claim documents so the system captures and validates the required data.

Acceptance. Data capture above ninety five percent accuracy, linked to the right policy, missing fields flagged with a clear message.

Epic. Fraud and anomaly signals
Early signals for suspicious patterns and expired or invalid items.
Epic. Experience and reporting
Clear status views for brokers and claimants and leadership dashboards for outcomes.

Roadmap

A simple three phase path that balances learning with speed.

Phase one. MVP for auto
Document intake, validation, status views, and a small pilot with real users.
Phase two. Expand to home
Add home claims, stronger fraud signals, and workflow refinements from pilot feedback.
Phase three. Scale and insights
Roll out across regions and launch leadership dashboards and continuous improvement loops.
